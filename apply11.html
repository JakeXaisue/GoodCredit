How to build good credit.

We live in a world where having a good credit can go a long way. A good credit gives you more opportunity in life vs. a bad credit. With a good credit, banks are more willing to loan you the money you need whether it be for a car, a mortgage, a boat, or personal reasons. Building your credit at a young age (18yrs) is best. There are many different ways to acquire a good credit. When it comes to building a good credit, it can be tricky. Here are some tips that can help you get started:

  • Always make sure you pay your bills on time.
  • Limit yourself up to two credit cards at a time.
  • Only take out credit lines that you can afford to pay back.
  • Having too much credit inquires can have a negative impact on your credit.
  • Use a co-signer with a good credit and make sure it is okay by them. This can help boost your credit and lower your interest rate.
